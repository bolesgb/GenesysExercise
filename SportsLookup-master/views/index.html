Sports Catalog

Introduction:

There are a ton of sports teams out in the world, tracking allof them could be hard if you have many sports you enjoy. That is where our system comes in. Our goal is to have a localized place for fans to come and view roasters, owners, stadiums, and managers of specific teams. All of this data will be stored in our application and will allow users to navigate and searchthe data based multiple different factors. For more information reguarding how to use the system please look below.

Functionality Over View:

The tabs at the top of the page on the nav-bar will allow you to select how you want to search through the data. This means if you know a stadium that the team uses to play, you can search by the stadium name or location under the stadium tab. So and so forth for each tab. This means that each tab may return some information that are cross sport. The useful part of our application is that whenyou click on one of the entries returned then we will populate a page for you based on the information that you clicked. This will then link to other information that is relevant to the item that you clicked. This information for the stadium could be the sport it is used for, what teams play there, and so on. Each of this linking information will also be clickable and bring you to another relevant page. This is how our application differs from other applications like it. We take the time to make sure that all of the information is specific to what you as the user are looking for.
